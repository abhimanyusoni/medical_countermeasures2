Who We Are

MedicalCountermeasures.gov facilitates communication between federal government agencies and public stakeholders to enhance the Nation's public health emergency preparedness.

Objectives of medicalcountermeasures.gov

Communication

To provide external stakeholders with a single point of entry to the U.S. government to discuss products that treat or prevent harm from a threat. Stakeholders with medicalcountermeasures will be routed to the most appropriate department within the federal government for consideration.
